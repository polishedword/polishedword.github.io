Who we are

Polished Word is a professional editing and proofreading business based in Brisbane, Australia.

We are committed to providing high quality editing and proofreading services with speed and efficiency.

Philip Peterson

Director

Philip graduated from Queensland University of Technology in 2006, obtaining his BFA in Creative Writing Production with High Distinctions in Editing, Proofreading and Corporate Communications.

Since then Philip has undertaken a number of roles, particularly in the not-for-profit sector, and, in addition to his role at Polished Word, is presently the Appeals & Direct Mail Coordinator for one of the largest not-for-profit organisations in Queensland.
